Lincoln Series

For this project, I made a series for the city of Lincoln highlighting the interesting spots within the city. My goal was to create something that reflected the art deco style. To get the style I was going for, I realized that art deco was very blocky with clean lines and simple illustrations. I decided to try this and keep it simple while layering the illustrations to add some depth.
