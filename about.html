About Me

Hey, I’m Kayleen Scott, a graphic designer and Colorado native. My passion for design revolves around branding, packaging, and illustration. Creativity has always been my way of expressing myself. Like many designers I first developed my love for creativity through drawing. And I have continued this pursuit through the mediums of painting, pottery, knitting, crocheting, and now sewing. One thing has remained constant in all my creative pursuits: flowers. starting my sophomore year of high school when I became obsessed with photographing flowers. Since then, floral elements have naturally woven themselves into my art and design projects. As you explore my portfolio, you’ll see how nature, especially flowers, plays a major role in my artistic style. I’m excited to share my work with you!
