Coco Planets

For this project, I designed a cereal box featuring astronaut bears, inspired by the shape and color of the cereal itself. The name Coco Planets naturally led me to create a space theme, with the bears floating in the colorful space. To make the sky more playful and engaging, I added colorful stars, hearts, and other fun details. I also designed cute planets to match the other elements.
