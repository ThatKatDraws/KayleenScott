The Mill Graphic

For this project, the goal was to create a two-color graphic for a t-shirt. I chose to design a piece inspired by The Mill, a local coffee shop. Since I had recently worked on the Colorado poster above, I wanted to experiment with a floral border again. I also loved the idea of using a coffee cup as a vase—it was a fun and creative way to tie in the theme.
