Valentine's Project

This was a personal project I created for Valentine’s Day for me and my boyfriend. I had drawn myself before, and when I decided to draw him as well, I thought it would be fun to incorporate both of us into candy packaging. That idea eventually led to Kay n Jay’s Chocolates, a brand featuring our nick-names. I came across a hand-drawn font that I really liked, which inspired me to create the Kay n Jay’s Chocolates lettering by hand.
