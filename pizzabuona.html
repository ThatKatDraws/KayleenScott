Pizza Buona

For this project, I developed a full brand identity, including a logo, packaging design, and brand guide. My goal was to create packaging that felt complex but not overwhelming, and something that felt a little more pricey. To achieve this, I focused on balancing fun and illustrative designs with my own style. I chose a softer color palette paired with dynamic elements to bring a handcrafted yet refined feel.
